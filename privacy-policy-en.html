Information Collection and Use

Our applications are designed to operate primarily on your device.

Permissions Used by the Applications

Screen Recording Permission

Some applications require Screen Recording permission to provide functionality such as:

  • Screen watermark rendering
  • Screenshot capture
  • Screen recording
  • OCR text recognition
  • Menu bar analysis and management

Screen content is processed locally and is not transmitted to us.

Accessibility Permission

Accessibility permission may be required to:

  • Simulate user interactions
  • Control menu bar items
  • Execute automation features
  • Improve workflow efficiency

Accessibility-related information is processed locally and is never transmitted to us.

Automation Permission

Automation permission allows the application to interact with macOS applications such as:

  • Finder
  • System Events
  • Other user-authorized applications

Automation actions occur only when initiated by the user.

Files and Folders Access

Applications may request access to files and folders selected by the user for:

  • Saving screenshots
  • Exporting files
  • Importing user content
  • Reading or writing authorized files

Only files explicitly selected or authorized by the user are accessible.

Clipboard Access

Clipboard access may be used for:

  • Copying screenshots
  • Copying generated content
  • Importing images or text from the clipboard

Clipboard contents are processed locally and are not transmitted to us.

Microphone Permission

If enabled by the user, microphone access may be used during:

  • Screen recording
  • Audio recording

Audio data remains on the user's device and is not transmitted to us.

Notification Permission

Some applications may request permission to display notifications for reminders, alerts, status updates, or workflow-related events.

Notification data is generated locally and is not shared with us.

Data Storage

All application-generated content is stored locally on your device unless a specific feature explicitly states otherwise.

Data Sharing

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
