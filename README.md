# Massimo Yao Privacy & Support Pages

Static GitHub Pages site containing privacy, terms, and support documents for apps published by Massimo Yao.

## Main pages

- `index.html` — bilingual document hub
- `hidedesk-privacy-policy-en.html` — HideDesk privacy policy in English
- `hidedesk-privacy-policy-zh.html` — HideDesk privacy policy in Simplified Chinese
- `privacy-policy-aegismark-en.html` — AegisMark privacy policy
- `privacy-policy-en.html` / `privacy-policy-zh.html` — general privacy policies
- `terms-of-service-en.html` / `terms-of-service-zh.html` — terms of service
- `support-en.html` / `support-zh.html` — support pages

The site is dependency-free and can be previewed with any local static-file server.
